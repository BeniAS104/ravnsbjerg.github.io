Rules

• Smoking is prohibited.

• Always wear appropriate footwear. We do not allow bare feet here.

• Put the gym equipment back where it belongs, weights, handles and alike.

• Use the disinfectant sprays on the surface of the equipment and wipe with paper.

• It is not permitted to take equipment home or outside the cardio/weight room.

• While playing loud music is generally allowed please be mindful of the other gym-goers and the people living above.

• The gym is only for current residents of the dorm. Sharing access in your company or without is strictly forbidden.

• Do not take pictures/videos of other people without their consent.

• If you break it - you buy it! Any damage to the facilities will need to be compensated for. Handle our equipment with care. Neglect of this rule will result in a permanent ban from the gym.

• Using the gym is at your own risk.

Location

The Gym is located in the basement of building 34.
