Laundry room

Opening Hours

7:00 - 23:00
every day

Rules

• Using laundry room is free of charge.

• Residents must book a place in queue beforehand.

• The laundry room is not a storage for clothes.

• All clothes lying around outside a laundry bag/basket will be removed every time the cleaning have been done.

• If you are missing any clothes from the laundry, please contact the caretaker.

• When you leave the laundry room, please close the door.

Location

Laundry rooms are located in the basement of every building.
